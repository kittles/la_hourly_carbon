Los Angeles Carbon Emissions (tC) - 2011

This shows the hourly carbon emissions of 1km2 regions of Los Angeles. The data was collected by the Hestia Project, which has a website here.

The slider allows you to select which hour you are viewing. Clicking the auto play button makes the computer scrub through the hours of the dataset. The key at the bottom of the image shows how the colors correspond to tons of carbon emitted.
